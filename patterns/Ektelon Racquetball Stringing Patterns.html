O3 & O PORT RACQUETS: Start main string at top of racquet (Method B) except certain AirO models. Many will require a flat washer to prevent the Start Pin from pulling through the large O ports. We have included a washer in your Maintenance Packet for this purpose.Â  Also, to keep the string on the correct side of the O port, use the Brake Pin to lock your turntable in place while tensioning.Â  This usually applies to the first half of the cross strings only. Some O port models require cross string to start on specific side. Refer to markings on racquet.
